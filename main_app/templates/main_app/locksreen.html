Secure Passwords

Secure Passwords are key to ensure your computer, accounts, and identity are secure. Here are some rules to ensure your password is secure!

  1. Use a different password for each account
  2. Use a password manager to keep track of your passwords
  3. Dont leave passwords on stickie notes etc.
  4. Dont use "password", "1234", or any placeholder or obvious passwords
  5. Use a variety of characters in your password
